## Mission & Vision

> "Technology onboarding should be a magical, seamless experience for both employer & employee."

> "Making computers simple, easy and ready to work for every human."

## Primary Tagline

**"Run your business and we handle the tech."**

## What Equipped Does

Equipped is a **B2B IT asset management and device provisioning platform** that specializes in:

1. **Employee Tech Onboarding** - Getting new hires set up with devices and software from day one
2. **IT Asset Management** - Tracking company devices, equipment, and tech inventory
3. **IT Spend Monitoring** - Keeping track of technology expenses across the organization
4. **Device Provisioning** - Delivering pre-configured, work-ready devices to employees anywhere

## Value Propositions

1. **"The easiest way to get your team the tech they need wherever they are"**
2. **Keep track of your assets and IT spend**
3. **Keep your team fully equipped and productive**
4. **Eliminate friction in employee onboarding**
5. **Personalized service ensuring employees have necessary devices and software from day one**

## Target Audience

- **HR Teams** - Streamlining new employee onboarding
- **IT Departments** - Reducing manual device setup workload
- **Operations Managers** - Asset tracking and inventory management
- **Remote-First Companies** - Getting devices to distributed teams globally
- **Growing Startups & SMBs** - Scaling tech operations without scaling IT headcount
- **Finance/Procurement** - Visibility into IT spend

## Core Problems Solved

1. **Slow Employee Onboarding** - New hires waiting days/weeks for equipment
2. **Manual IT Setup** - IT teams spending hours configuring each device
3. **Asset Visibility** - Not knowing who has what equipment
4. **IT Spend Chaos** - Lack of visibility into technology costs
5. **Remote Work Challenges** - Difficulty getting devices to distributed employees
6. **Productivity Delays** - New employees unable to work due to tech issues
